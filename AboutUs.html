About Us

Awaken Your Senses with Every Sip

"At Sanbak, we are passionate about bringing you an exceptional coffee experience. Located in the heart of Jakarta, we are dedicated to providing a warm and inviting atmosphere where coffee lovers can gather, relax, and indulge in the art of coffee."

- Dionisius Sylvester Wime, General Manager


Our Story

Founded in 2022, our coffee shop was born out of a shared love for coffee and a desire to create a community space that celebrates the rich and diverse coffee culture of Jakarta. We believe that a cup of coffee has the power to connect people, spark conversations, and inspire creativity. With this vision in mind, we set out to create a coffee shop that goes beyond serving great coffee - we aim to create meaningful experiences for our customers.


Quality Coffee

At Sanbak, we are committed to sourcing and serving only the finest, ethically-sourced coffee beans. Our dedicated team of expert baristas undergoes rigorous training to ensure that each cup of coffee is crafted with precision and care. Whether you prefer a classic espresso, a velvety latte, or a hand-poured artisanal brew, we have something to satisfy every coffee connoisseur's palate.


Warm Atmosphere

Step into our coffee shop, and you'll be greeted by a cozy and inviting ambiance. Our space is designed to provide a comfortable environment for you to unwind, catch up with friends, or find a quiet corner to work. We believe that coffee is best enjoyed with good company, so we've created communal tables and cozy nooks to encourage conversations and connections.

Delicious Treats

Pair your coffee with a selection of delectable treats from our in-house bakery. From freshly baked pastries to indulgent cakes and cookies, our menu offers a range of options to satisfy your cravings. We take pride in using high-quality ingredients and crafting our treats with love, ensuring that each bite is a moment of pure delight.


Community Engagement

We are more than just a coffee shop, we are an active member of the Jakarta community. We strive to give back by supporting local farmers and organizations that share our values. We regularly host events such as cupping sessions, coffee workshops, and live music performances, creating opportunities for coffee enthusiasts to deepen their knowledge and connect with like-minded individuals.


Visit Us

We invite you to come and experience the world of coffee at Sanbak. Whether you're a coffee aficionado, a casual coffee drinker, or simply looking for a welcoming space to relax, we have something special for you. Our knowledgeable staff is here to guide you through our menu and help you discover your perfect cup of coffee. We look forward to serving you soon!


Join us at Sanbak in Jakarta, and let us be your destination for exceptional coffee and unforgettable moments.


We look forward to serving you soon!
